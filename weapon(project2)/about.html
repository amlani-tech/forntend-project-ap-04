About DRDO

The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) is the premier R&D organization under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. It is responsible for developing cutting-edge defense technologies including missiles, combat vehicles, radars, electronics, and more. With over 50 laboratories across India, DRDO empowers the nation's armed forces with indigenous solutions for land, air, sea, and space.

About PXE, Balasore

The Proof and Experimental Establishment (PXE), located in Balasore, Odisha, is a critical laboratory of DRDO. PXE specializes in testing and evaluating armament stores such as artillery shells, bombs, rockets, and aerial delivery systems. It plays a vital role in verifying the performance, accuracy, and safety of weapons before they are inducted into service.

Indian Army

Land-based systems such as tanks, rifles, and rocket launchers. DRDO provides advanced protection and firepower capabilities.

Indian Navy

Sea-based weapons including torpedoes, sonar systems, and shipboard missiles, tested for combat and resilience.

Indian Airforce

High-speed aerial systems including missiles, radars, avionics, and smart bombs tailored for air dominance.
