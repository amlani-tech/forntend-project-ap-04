Services

At EverGreen, we provide a range of services to connect people with impactful environmental and social initiatives, making it easy to contribute towards a better future.

Event Discovery & Registration

  • 🌿 Discover verified eco-friendly and social events near you.
  • 🌿 Tree plantation drives, cleanups, and awareness workshops.
  • 🌿 Food distribution and community aid programs.
  • 🌿 Recycling and waste management campaigns.
  • 🌿 Search, filter, and register for events that suit your schedule.

Volunteer Management

  • 🌿 Connect volunteers with meaningful causes.
  • 🌿 Join as an individual or create your own team.
  • 🌿 Track your contributions and event history.
  • 🌿 Earn recognition badges and awards.

  • 🌿 Help NGOs post and promote their events.

  • 🌿 Expand reach to a larger, active audience.

  • 🌿 Coordinate school and college programs.
  • 🌿 Partner with media for wider reach.
  • 🌿 Provide campaign toolkits and resources.

Community Welfare Services

  • 🌿 Organize health camps for underserved areas.
  • 🌿 Support education for underprivileged children.

Sustainable Living Guidance

  • 🌿 Promote eco-friendly household practices.
  • 🌿 Share DIY recycling and upcycling ideas.

Awareness & Education
